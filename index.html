Sirod's Portal Adventure

Sirod, an 18-year-old from Brooklyn, was walking home when a glowing portal appeared in the middle of the street...

Without hesitation, Sirod jumped in. He landed in a futuristic city floating in the sky. Hovercars zoomed past. A robot asked, “Are you the chosen one?”

Sirod turned and sprinted. But as he reached home, everything had changed — his apartment was gone, replaced by a jungle. “Uh… Brooklyn?” he muttered.

The robots crowned Sirod as their leader. Turns out, he was a time-traveling legend in their records. He now rules Neon City — with a jetpack throne and all.

Sirod built a treehouse, trained monkeys to box, and became the King of Jungle Brooklyn. No WiFi, but a monkey named Carl makes great pancakes.
